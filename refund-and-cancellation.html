REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

At Zenopay Solutions Private Limited, we aim to provide smooth and secure payment services.

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains the conditions under which refunds may or may not be processed.

1. Refunds for Payment Transactions

A refund may be considered only if:

  • • The transaction was failed, but the amount was deducted
  • • The payment was processed twice (duplicate payment)
  • • Amount was deducted but not received by the merchant

2. Refund Process

If you believe a refund is required, you must provide:

  • • Transaction ID
  • • Date & time of transaction
  • • Amount paid
  • • Reason for refund request
  • • Any supporting proof/screenshots

Send refund requests to:

Email: zenopaypvt@gmail.com

3. Refund Timeline

Once a valid request is received:

  • • Refund processing time: 3-5 business days
  • • Bank transfer reflection time: 5-7 business days (may vary by bank)

Zenopay is not responsible for delays caused by banks or third-party systems.

4. Cancellation Policy

Since Zenopay provides payment processing services:

  • • Transactions cannot be cancelled after they are successfully processed.
  • • If a merchant agrees to cancel an order, the refund will be issued by the merchant, not Zenopay.

5. No Refund in These Cases

Refunds will not be given for:

  • • Successful transactions
  • • Customer mistakes such as wrong number, wrong merchant, etc.
  • • Change of mind after payment
  • • Fraudulent or unauthorized use of user account
  • • Merchant disputes (handled directly by the merchant)
